Our Shop

A Local Card Shop Built For Collectors Of Every Level

Whether you are chasing your next big hit, building a personal collection, helping your kid get into the hobby, or looking to sell cards you no longer want, 5th Quarter Sports Cards & Memorabilia gives you a place to shop, ask questions, and enjoy the hobby.

We buy, sell, and trade sports cards across baseball, basketball, football, and more. You can stop in for sealed products, browse singles, pick up supplies, ask about PSA grading, or talk with our team about what you have and what it may be worth.

Why 5th Quarter

Fair Deals. Real Knowledge. A Shop You Can Trust.

Buying and selling cards online can be uncertain. You may have to deal with shipping, fees, delays, scams, changing values, or sellers who do not know the hobby.

At 5th Quarter, you can work with a local team that understands sports, cards, collectors, and the market. We take time to research values, explain what we are seeing, and help people make confident decisions.

If you are buying, we want you to enjoy what you pick up. If you are selling, we want the process to feel fair, clear, and simple. If you are new to collecting, we want you to feel comfortable asking questions.

The Problem

Selling Cards Should Not Feel Like A Guessing Game

If you are not watching the market every day, it can be hard to know what your cards are worth. Online listings can be confusing. Sold prices can vary. Condition matters. Player demand changes. Grading potential can change the conversation.

You should not have to figure it all out alone.

At 5th Quarter, we make the process more straightforward. We look at what you have, research the value, and explain the offer so you can decide what makes sense.

The Process

How Selling Or Trading Works

1

Send Photos Or Stop By

Text or message photos of your cards, or bring them into the shop during business hours.

2

We Research The Value

Our team reviews the cards, looks at available market information, and considers condition, demand, and resale value.

3

You Get A Fair Offer

If the cards are a fit, we make an offer. If you accept, you can get paid without waiting on auctions, shipping, or online buyers.

Why Local

Why Sell To A Local Card Shop Instead Of Online?

Selling online can work, but it can also take time. You may need to photograph every card, write listings, answer messages, pay fees, ship safely, handle returns, and wait for payment.

Selling locally gives you another option.

With 5th Quarter, you can talk with real people who know the hobby, get a research-based offer, and move forward without the extra steps of selling online.

Trust

Fair Offers Start With Honest Research

We know sellers want to feel confident that they are being treated fairly. That is why we do not want the process to feel rushed or unclear.

Our goal is to look at the cards, understand the current market, and make an offer based on what we can reasonably pay. Sometimes that means buying. Sometimes it means helping you understand what you have. Either way, you will get a straightforward conversation.
